Fun Things to Do Around Medfield Massachusetts

Medfield, Massachusetts is a charming New England town with plenty to do for visitors and locals alike. Whether you are in the mood for outdoor adventure or looking for a place to relax and take in some local culture, Medfield has something for everyone. From historic sites to mouthwatering restaurants, let’s explore some of the fun things that this quaint small town has to offer.

No visit to Medfield would be complete without exploring its rich history and heritage. Tourists can learn about the area by visiting the 19th century Victorian-style homes that line Main Street or see what daily life was like during Colonial times at one of many museums in the area. For a more active experience, head out on foot or bike along scenic trails around Bowman Pond where you can spot wildlife while enjoying fresh air and beautiful views.


If shopping is more your thing, there are plenty of options available ranging from local boutiques selling handmade goods to larger stores offering everything from clothing to home décor items. If it's good food you're after then look no further than downtown Medfield where you can find an array of delicious eateries serving up classic American dishes as well as international fare such as Italian, Mexican, Indian and Chinese cuisine. So come explore this delightful little community that offers something for everyone!

Entertainment: The Norwood Theatre

The Norwood Theatre is a great spot for entertainment in the Medfield, Massachusetts area. It has been around since 1936, and it continues to be an anchor of local culture. The theatre offers something special; it's not just your typical movie theater experience – from the moment you walk inside its doors, you can feel the charm and nostalgia that still linger within the walls of this classic building.


From comedy shows to musicals and plays, there are plenty of events taking place at the Norwood Theatre to keep locals entertained year-round. And no matter what type of show or event you’re looking for, you’re sure to find something that appeals to your taste here. Plus, with their affordable ticket prices and concessions stand stocked with popcorn and candy, going out to take in a show at the Norwood Theatre won't break your bank either!

Taking In A Show At The Norwood Theatre

No matter what kind of performance you decide to attend at The Norwood Theatre, you can be sure you'll have a memorable time. The atmosphere within the theatre itself is intimate yet cozy; perfect for enjoying whatever show may come your way while taking advantage of all the amenities offered by this venerable establishment. Outdoor Recreation: Rocky Woods, Mass Audubon's Broadmoor Wildlife Sanctuary, Noon Hill Reservation, And Hinkley Swim Pond

If you're looking for outdoor recreation in and around Medfield, Massachusetts, there are plenty of places to explore. Rocky Woods offers 650 acres of trails over the Charles River Valley with a variety of terrain and historic sites. Mass Audubon's Broadmoor Wildlife Sanctuary is just minutes away from town and provides 1,000 acres of habitats for observing birds, mammals and amphibians as well as miles of scenic walking trails.


Hinkley Swim Pond has been part of the community since 1908 and allows swimming, fishing, boating and picnicking along its shores. Noon Hill Reservation is another great spot to take in some nature; it includes more than 325 acres of woodlands, wetlands and meadows with several hiking paths throughout.


No matter where your outdoor adventures lead you around Medfield, there are so many options that provide hours of fun exploration through beautiful scenery. Whether it's taking a leisurely stroll on one of the numerous trails or having a picnic at Hinkley swim pond while watching boats drift by on the water – these areas offer something special to visitors no matter what their interests may be.


With all this natural beauty right outside your door, get ready to experience some amazing sights! And when you're done exploring the outdoors, don't forget about observing the wildlife at Mass Audubon's Broadmoor Wildlife Sanctuary...

Observing The Wildlife At Mass Audubon's Broadmoor Wildlife Sanctuary

Mass Audubon's Broadmoor Wildlife Sanctuary is a great place to observe nature and the abundance of wildlife that inhabit Medfield, Massachusetts. Located on North Street in Sherborn, visitors can enjoy hiking trails as well as birdwatching opportunities. The sanctuary also provides educational programs for families, school groups, and adults about their local flora and fauna.


The sanctuary has many different habitats including meadows, wetlands, woodlands and ponds. This diverse range of environments allows for a variety of species to be present at any given time such as songbirds, reptiles and amphibians, butterflies and dragonflies. On certain days during spring migration season there are guided tours available which provide information about the birds that pass through the area as they make their way north for nesting season.


Observing the wildlife at Mass Audubon’s Broadmoor Wildlife Sanctuary is an ideal activity for those looking to get outside with friends or family while still being able to learn something new. With its diverse ecosystems it offers plenty of opportunities to observe some truly amazing creatures in their natural habitat. From this point one could move onto swimming and relaxing at Hinkley Swim Pond just down the road – another fantastic outdoor recreation option in Medfield!
